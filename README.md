# AIOS-in-Claude-Capstone-Project

## Overview of what we're trying to achieve

We looked at having AI employees that can perform complex stacks across systems, and we used a third-party provider called Spinable to achieve that. Now we are looking to achieve the same results but building our own AI operating system with interconnectivity with different applications, scheduled tasks, and the ability to communicate via channels like WhatsApp or Telegram with these.

We want to replicate the idea of full fledged AI Employees we saw on Spinnable, but now om our own setup.
